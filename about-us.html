Our Inspiring Journey

IRONHEX was founded in **Tunisia in 2025** by two visionary female engineers: one a cybersecurity expert, the other an IoT innovator. Their shared passion for technology and a commitment to creating a safer, more connected world led to the birth of a company dedicated to pushing the boundaries of digital security and intelligent automation.

From humble beginnings, their combined expertise quickly forged a powerful synergy, building IRONHEX into a leader in providing robust, cutting-edge solutions for businesses navigating the complexities of the modern digital landscape. Their story is a testament to innovation, resilience, and the power of diverse perspectives in technology.
